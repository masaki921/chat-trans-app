About ChatTranslate

ChatTranslate is a messaging app that automatically translates your messages in real-time using AI. Communicate naturally with anyone, regardless of the language they speak.

FAQ

How does translation work?

Messages are automatically translated using AI (Google Gemini). When you send a message, the recipient sees both the translated text and the original message. Translation happens instantly in real-time.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > scroll to the bottom > tap "Delete Account". You will be asked to confirm by typing "DELETE". This action is permanent and will remove all your data.

How do I report or block a user?

In a chat room, tap the menu button (…) in the top right corner. You can choose to block or report the user. You can also long-press on a specific message to report it. Blocked users can be managed in Settings > Blocked Users.

What languages are supported?

ChatTranslate supports translation between many languages. The app interface is available in English, Japanese, and Chinese. The AI translation supports a wide range of languages.
